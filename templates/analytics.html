AI Insights

  • Credit Score is the strongest positive indicator of loan repayment.
  • Customers with a higher Debt-to-Income Ratio have significantly higher EMI default probability.
  • Previous Delinquency History increases the chance of loan default.
  • Higher Interest Rate moderately increases repayment risk.
  • Loan Amount alone has relatively low influence on loan repayment.
  • Previous repayment behaviour is a stronger predictor than customer age or gender.
